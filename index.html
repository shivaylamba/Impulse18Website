MAITHacks Themes
MaitHacks Rules

To check your innovative mind and coding skills grab the opportunity of MAIT HACKS. The event consists of two rounds, starting from 7th March 2018 at 9 pm for the next 36 hours.
Participants need to register in teams of maximum 4.

ROUND 1

It will be an online round where participants will get two random keywords generated from the website on 7th March from 9 pm onwards.
The teams would be required to submit their project ideas based on both of the keywords provided and upload on our website before 12 pm of 8th March 2018.
The results of Round 1 will be sent to the shortlisted teams via email on the same day by 6 pm. Selection will be based on the originality and usefulness of the idea submitted.

Round 2

The Second Round will be held in the MAIT Campus on 9th March - 9am onwards.
In this round, selected teams from round 1 would have to come for the project pitching and demonstration .



Get ready for prizes worth Rs 1 lakh, Cash prize of Rs 20,000 , internship and incubation offers, Certificates, .tech domains to everyone and much more. Come on guys, do it for the cookies, if nothing else.

CodeSaga Rules

If you have great logical and problem solving skills,come on! This event could help in your coffee bills.
The event will commence at 9 am on 10th March 2018.

ROUND 1

Round 1 would be a 2 hour problem solving competitive round. Participants can use any language of their choice.

Round 2

Top 8 Participants of Round 1 will compete against each other in a series of knockout rounds. Winners of this round will also become the finalists for a coding event in IIT Roorkee



The winners will be awarded with exciting prizes and internship offers. Top participants will be provided with Certificates of Appreciation.

Pixel Rules

Time to make lives simpler! If you got the talent Participate in this event or you will lament
UI/UX Designing will consist of 2 rounds.

ROUND 1

It will be an online round. The participants need to send their best works at iosdmait@iosd.tech
Participants with the best work will be selected for Round 2.

Round 2

This will be an offline round. It will be held in the MAIT Campus on 9 March 2018 from 2-5 p.m. The selected participants will have to design a UI/UX on the themes which will be given on the spot.



Participants can choose to design for either mobile app or website.
Plagiarised designs will not be accepted. Participants should carry their own laptops.

Designers, time to get down to work!

  • MAITHacks

    Can students from branches apart from CSE/IT participate? Also, can hardware models like that of IOT, block chain be included in the project submission?

    Students from any branch can take part in the competition. Yes, the project submission can include both hardware and software components.

    Do the teams need to send a part of the working model while submitting project ideas for the online round?

    No, it is not necessary to submit a prototype of the working project along with the project idea. But the project idea must be elaborate and well explained.

    Can the students selected for 2nd round complete the project on campus and do they need to carry their laptops?

    No, the campus round is only for project pitching and demonstration and the working project must be at least 70% completed before bringing it to the campus round. Yes, the students need to carry their own laptops.

  • Pixel

    No, any relevant theme can be chosen. But the idea must be unique and plagiarized designs will not be accepted.

  • CodeSaga

    Will the first round take place in the college campus? What kind of questions will be provided in the round 2?

    Yes the first round will take place in the college campus itself. Location details will be provided on the spot. All the details will be provided on the spot. But for solving the questions, as with the first round, the questions can be attempted in any language.

    Do the participants need to bring their own laptops for the event?

    No, the event will take place in college labs. So there is no need for laptops.

    Yes, participation certificates will be provided for all the events including the workshop.
